Privacy Policy for HearThai

Effective Date: November 24, 2025

HearThai (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. We do not require account registration to use the app.

2. App Tracking & Ads

We do not track you across other apps or websites.

We use Apple's SKAdNetwork for anonymous ad attribution. This process is handled entirely by the iOS operating system and does not share your identity or device ID with us or third parties.

3. Subscriptions

All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.

4. Local Data

Your learning progress and settings are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data may be lost unless backed up via your device's standard iCloud backup system.
